Getting Started

  1. Open the Claude Usage app and tap Sign In to Claude.ai.
  2. Log in with your Anthropic account in the browser sheet that appears, then tap Close.
  3. Tap Refresh to verify your usage loads correctly.
  4. Long-press your home screen, add a widget, and search for Claude Usage.
  5. Choose small, medium, or large size, and optionally pick a chart style by tapping Edit Widget.

Frequently Asked Questions

The widget says "Sign in to see usage." What do I do?

Open the Claude Usage app and tap Sign In to Claude.ai. You need to complete sign-in once so the app can store your session securely.

The widget shows stale data. How do I force a refresh?

Open the app and tap Refresh. iOS limits widget refresh rates; the widget automatically updates approximately every 15 minutes.

I signed in but the app still shows "Signed out."

Make sure you complete the full login flow on the Claude.ai page before tapping Close. If the issue persists, try signing out and back in again.

Which Claude plans does this work with?

This widget works with any Claude.ai account that has visible usage limits, including Claude Pro and team plans. Free accounts may not have usage-limit data exposed by the API.

Does this app store my credentials?

Your Claude.ai session cookie is stored securely in the iOS Keychain — the same system used by Apple's own apps and password manager. It is never sent anywhere other than directly to claude.ai. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I remove my data?

Tap Sign Out in the app. This immediately deletes your session key from the Keychain. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data.
